Section 01

Privacy Policy

At Zahan Store, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or make a purchase from us.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, shipping/billing address, payment information
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on site, referring website
  • Cookies: We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfill your orders
  • To communicate with you about your orders and account
  • To improve our website and services
  • To send marketing communications (if you've opted in)
  • To prevent fraud and enhance security

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with payment processors (PayPal, Razorpay, Stripe), shipping carriers, service providers, and when required by law.

4. Data Security

We implement SSL encryption, secure storage, and regular security assessments to protect your data. No method of transmission is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

  • Right to access the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to request deletion of your data
  • Right to opt-out of marketing communications
  • Right to restrict or object to processing

To exercise these rights, please contact us.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to remember preferences, analyse traffic, and deliver targeted ads (if consented). You can manage cookies via your browser settings.

7. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect their personal information.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The updated version will be posted on our website with the effective date.

Section 03

Return & Refund

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. Here's everything you need to know about returns and refunds.

1. Physical Products

Eligibility

  • Returns accepted within 14 days of delivery, unused and in original condition.
  • Items must be in original packaging with tags attached.
  • Customers cover return shipping costs unless the item is defective or incorrect.

Refund Timeline

  • Refunds processed within 7–10 business days of receiving the return.
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method.

2. Digital Products

  • Digital products are non-refundable once access has been granted.
  • Exceptions are made for verified technical issues that prevent access.
  • Contact within 48 hours if you experience issues.

3. Cancellations

  • Orders can be cancelled within 24 hours of placement.
  • Once dispatched, the return policy applies instead.

4. How to Initiate a Return

Email with your order number and reason for return. Our team will respond within 2–3 business days.
